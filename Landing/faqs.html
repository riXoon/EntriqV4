We believe that ENTRIQ empowers Quezon City University (QCU) entrepreneurship students by providing a platform to showcase their creativity, expand their market reach, and grow their businesses, all while fostering a supportive and thriving community.

Frequently Asked Questions

ENTRIQ is an innovative e-commerce platform to help Quezon City University (QCU) entrepreneurship students promote, showcase, and sell their products more effectively. It provides a digital marketplace that enhances their business growth and market reach.
ENTRIQ is exclusively designed for:
  • QCU Entrepreneurship Students – To list, promote, and manage their products.
  • QCU Students – To explore and purchase products created by fellow student entrepreneurs.
ENTRIQ offers a wide variety of products created by QCU student entrepreneurs, including:
  • Snacks and beverages
  • Personal care items
  • Accessories and handcrafted products
  • Other student-made goods
Currently, ENTRIQ only supports Cash on Delivery (COD) for all transactions. Online payment gateways are not yet integrated.
Entrepreneurs can generate QR codes for their business profiles and products, making it easier for potential customers to scan and access their listings quickly.
No, ENTRIQ does not provide large-scale delivery services. All transactions and pickups will be conducted within the QCU campus.
